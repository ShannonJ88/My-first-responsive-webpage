About Me

Posted July 17, 2017

My name is Shannon Julion and I love reading Computer Science books, and I have created this web site to show everyone some books that will become my favorite. A little information about myself, I currently work as an Academic advisor at the University of Illinois at Chicago. I have a Bachelor's of Arts degree in Psychology, and a Master's of Education degree with a concentration in Youth Development. Below are the links to the schools I attended.

  • University of Missouri
  • University of Illinois at Chicago
  • University of Illinois at Springfield

In my spare time, I love to travel and workout. I try to workout at least six times a week. I love looking up information about living a more healthy lifestyle and anything tech related. I also have a passion for reading and learning new things. I originally was interested in pursuing Math/Computer Science during Undergrad but I struggled with wanting to do more for others by working in the Education field. I also had limited knowledge about anything STEM related back in 2006. I just knew that I loved Math. I told myself I wanted to make a bigger impact on youth, so I then began a journey serving in various roles in educational settings. I was a High School College Counselor, College Retention Coach, and Admissions Counselor. I then decided that I should never give up on my dreams and finally decided to actively pursue learning more about computer/software development and Web Design. I am currently a Computer Science Student at the University of Illinois at Springfield. I want to eventually figure out how I can merge Computer Science and education together. I also want to motivate others to never give up on their dreams! The sky is the limit. I hope you enjoy my web site. Please feel free to contact me by clicking on the contact link on the navigation bar.
